About me

Hi! my name's Czhiaztee Raime B. Tingcungco , a first-year Bachelor of Science in Computer Science student at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila.

What drives me to explore Computer science is the thrill of seeing code — words and syntax — come together to create a functioning systems . I am someone who learns best through hands-on activities, thrives on curiosity, and enjoys through digital and creative designs.

I have gathered skills in multimedia editing, especially using Adobe After Effects for motion graphics and Canva Pro for custom designs. These tools help me visualize and creatively express my ideas. which is why I'm particularly interested in front-end development and hope to deepen my skills in UI and website design as I grow in this field.
